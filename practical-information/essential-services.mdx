# Essential Services & Important Contacts

Understanding how to access key services makes daily life smoother.

## Banking Services

### Opening Hours

**Standard:**
- 9 AM - 5 PM Monday-Friday
- Closed Saturday-Sunday
- Lunch break varies (usually 12-1:30 PM)
- Some branches close briefly

**Best time:** 9:30-11:30 AM (less crowded)

### Services Available

**Basic banking:**
- Deposits/withdrawals
- Transfers
- Card issuance
- Account inquiries
- Bill payments

**Business services:**
- Business checking accounts
- Wire transfers
- International transfers
- Loan applications

### Major Banks in Bodrum

**Garanti Bank:**
- Largest private bank
- Excellent app
- English support
- Multiple Bodrum locations

**İşbank:**
- State bank
- Reliable
- Good service
- Multiple locations

**Akbank:**
- Private bank
- Modern facilities
- English support
- Several locations

**Ziraatbank:**
- Smaller presence
- Friendly service
- Limited English

**HSBC Turkey:**
- International bank
- Premium services
- Better expat support
- Limited branches

## Government Services

### Municipality (Belediye)

**Services:**
- Utilities connections (water, electricity - actually at utility office but coordinated through)
- Residence certificates
- Business licenses
- Building permits
- Local taxes

**Address:** Main municipality office in Bodrum center
**Hours:** 9 AM - 5 PM M-F
**Contact:** Local number (ask at hotel/through expat networks)
**Tip:** Go early, bring multiple document copies

### Police/Security Services

**Local Police (Polis):**
- Traffic issues
- Reporting crimes
- Document verification
- General inquiries

**Neighborhood chief (Muhtar):**
- More accessible than police
- Local issues
- Community problems
- Can direct to proper office

**Tourist Police:**
- Specialized for expat issues
- English spoken
- Helpful attitude
- Contact through main police number (155)

**Where:** Police stations throughout Bodrum

### Tax Office (Vergi Müdürlüğü)

**Services:**
- Tax ID registration (Vergi Numarası)
- Tax filing support
- Business tax questions
- Document validation

**Address:** Central tax office (ask at municipality or bank)
**Hours:** 9 AM - 5 PM M-F
**Pro tip:** Go Monday-Thursday (less crowded than Friday)

### Immigration Office (Göç İdaresi)

**Services:**
- Residence permit application
- Visa extensions
- Residency status

**Address:** Usually at police headquarters or separate office
**Hours:** 9 AM - 5 PM M-F
**Appointment:** Often available/recommended
**Phone:** Available for initial inquiries

### Commerce Chamber (Ticaret Odası)

**Services:**
- Business registration (KVK)
- Chamber membership info
- Business support
- Networking events

**Address:** Bodrum business district
**Hours:** 9 AM - 5 PM M-F
**Contact:** Look up "Bodrum Ticaret Odası"

## Postal Services

### Post Office (PTT)

**Services:**
- Mail sending/receiving
- Parcels
- Money transfers
- Bill payments
- Some banking services

**Main Branch:**
- Central Bodrum location
- Full services
- Longer hours sometimes

**Neighborhood branches:**
- Scattered throughout
- Basic services
- Shorter hours

**Hours:**
- Main: 8 AM - 6 PM M-F, limited Sat
- Branches: 9 AM - 5 PM M-F

**Cost:** Very affordable ($1-3 for basic letter internationally)

**Timeline:** 2-4 weeks international typically

### Courier Services

**DHL:**
- Bodrum location or nearby
- International service
- 3-7 day delivery typical
- Higher cost, reliable

**FedEx:**
- Limited presence
- International service
- Similar pricing/timeline

**UPS:**
- Limited presence  
- Similar services

**Contact:** Search online for local branch

## Healthcare Facilities

See [Healthcare System](/getting-started/healthcare) for details.

### Private Hospitals

**Bodrum Private Hospital:**
- Full services
- 24/7 emergency
- Modern equipment
- English staff available

**Hours:** 24/7
**Emergency:** Call 112 or go directly to emergency room

### Private Clinics

**Multiple throughout Bodrum:**
- Routine care
- Specialist appointments
- Lab tests
- Pharmacy on-site often

**Hours:** Usually 9 AM - 6 PM, some variation

**Finding doctors with English:**
- Ask hotel concierge
- Ask other expats
- Call clinic to confirm
- Tourist police can recommend

### Pharmacies

**Everywhere in Bodrum:**
- Green cross sign (Eczane)
- Most towns have 24/7 pharmacy schedule
- Excellent service
- Open 9 AM - 8 PM typical

**Weekend/night:**
- Pharmacies rotate duty
- Signs on doors show which are open
- Larger neighborhoods have 24 hour options

### 24-Hour Pharmacies

**Check with:**
- Local pharmacy (they'll direct you)
- Hotel concierge
- Police (in emergency)

### Tourist Information Office

**Services:**
- Visitor information
- Maps
- Event calendars
- Tour recommendations
- Hotel information

**Address:** Central Bodrum, near harbor
**Hours:** Seasonal variation (busier in summer)

### Hotel Concierge

**Often helpful for:**

- Taxi/transportation
- Medical referrals
- Service provider contacts
- Local connections

**Tip:** Befriend concierge - they know everything

## Embassies/Consulates

### Nearest Embassies (All in Istanbul)

**US Embassy:**
Istanbul office (serves Turkey including Bodrum region)
- Website: tr.usembassy.gov
- Phone: Available
- Consular services available

**UK Embassy:**
- Similar setup

**Canadian Embassy:**
- In Ankara (Consular in Istanbul)

**UAE/Qatar:**
- Istanbul missions
- Limited Bodrum presence

**Note:** Register with your embassy when you arrive. Easy online, gives them contact info for emergencies.

## Legal Services

### Turkish Lawyers (Avukat)

**Services:**
- Business legal matters
- Real estate
- Contracts
- Disputes
- Immigration issues

**Cost:** $30-100+ per hour depending on complexity

**Finding:**
- Referrals from other expats (best)
- Chamber of Commerce
- Google search
- Bank recommendations

**Vetting:**
- English speaking?
- Experience with expats?
- What's their specific expertise?
- Get fee agreement upfront

### Consular Services (If Needed)

**Contact your embassy for:**
- Document certifications
- Serious legal matters
- Major emergencies
- Visa issues

## Shopping & Services

### Laundry Services

**Where:**
- Laundromats (rare)
- Laundry services (more common)
- Ask landlord for recommendations

**Cost:** Reasonable ($5-15 per load typically with service)

### Haircut/Hairdressing

**Where:**
- Hairdressers throughout Bodrum
- Quality varies
- Ask other expats for recommendations

**Cost:** $10-30 typical (much cheaper than West)

### Car Repair & Maintenance

**Mechanic shops (Oto Elektrik, Oto Tamircisi):**
- Throughout Bodrum
- Quality varies
- Get recommendations
- Ask about warranty on parts

**Cost:** Much cheaper than Western countries

## Emergency Resources Reminder

**Critical numbers:**
- 112: Ambulance/Police/Fire (all emergencies)
- 155: Police non-emergency
- Your embassy: Saved in phone

**Important documents:**
- Keep copies at secure location
- Cloud backup of everything
- Know your insurance details
