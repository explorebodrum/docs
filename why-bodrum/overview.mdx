## What's Different From Dubai/Qatar?

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Pace of Life">
    **Gulf:** Fast-paced, 24/7 hustle culture
    
    **Bodrum:** Slower, beach-lifestyle pace. Schools close for summer, businesses take breaks. Time for personal pursuits.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Community">
    **Gulf:** Large expat bubbles, segregated communities
    
    **Bodrum:** Smaller, integrated expat communities. You'll interact with local Turks and international travelers.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Cost Structure">
    **Gulf:** High salaries offset high expenses
    
    **Bodrum:** Lower costs allow strategic living - work less, earn from remote jobs, or invest in business.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Work Culture">
    **Gulf:** Hierarchical, corporate-driven
    
    **Bodrum:** Entrepreneurial spirit, freelancing popular, business-friendly environment.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
